What is Hours?

CHECKD® Hours allows you to efficiently log your working-hours and tasks accordingly.
 

Overview:

Choose a project from the list generated, depending in your position. Add tasks as easy as sending SMS. You will be automatically checked out when leaving the project area.

Timesheets

Overview over your daily, monthy and total hours. View how long you have been working on different tasks. Easy integration towards other systems.

Back-End

Detailed information of your worskspace. See where you spent an excessive amount of time so that you can streamline.
