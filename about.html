About

CHECKD AS wishes to raise awareness regarding the enormous costs related to the various mistakes, errors and misplacements that occur in the construction industry on a daily basis.

We provide a simple and easy-to-understand method of regaining control through our cloud-based virtual toolkit, which allows users to log all critical and non-critical activities, file reports, coordinate subcontractors and track essential equipment.
